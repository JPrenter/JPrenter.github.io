A trusted voice in personal finance

Jack Prenter is an entrepreneur and personal finance expert. He is the CEO of Dollarwise and has served as chairman of the investment committee for a $250M+ fund.

His expertise and Dollarwise have been featured in The Globe and Mail, Nasdaq, Sage, Progress, Yahoo Finance, Consumer Affairs, MSN, CardRatings.com, EON, GoBankingRates, and many more.

His personal finance newsletter reaches 50,000 readers per month, where he shares all of the articles that he's quoted in. Through Dollarwise and his newsletter, he has helped thousands to save money and make smarter financial decisions.

Entrepreneur

Jack Prenter founded Dollarwise and continues to serve as CEO of the company. As a personal finance expert, he created their advanced systems for automated financial product comparison.

He studied Economics at the University of Nottingham, where he was on the board of both the Entrepreneurship Society and the Finance Club. These two pursuits would eventually combine and lead to the creation of Dollarwise.

Investor

He served as chairman for the $250M+ investment committee of Nexus Mutual, with an alternative investment mandate including digital assets. There, he analyzed new markets and emerging investment opportunities to generate sustainable returns.

Jack Prenter is an entrepreneur at heart. To support this community, he is investing in and consulting with startups, particularly in the financial industry.
